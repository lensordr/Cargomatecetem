Our Services

International Road Transport

We provide international road transport services across Europe, ensuring safe, timely, and efficient delivery of goods. Our operations are structured to support cross-border logistics with full visibility, coordination, and consistent communication throughout the transport process.

Full Truckload (FTL) & Dedicated Transport

CargoMate offers Full Truckload (FTL) and dedicated transport solutions tailored to specific route, timing, and capacity requirements. We support both regular and ad-hoc shipments, ensuring reliable execution, capacity availability, and operational control.

Less Than Truckload (LTL) Transport

For shipments that do not require full truck capacity, we provide Less Than Truckload (LTL) solutions through optimized consolidation and trusted partner networks. Our LTL services focus on efficiency, dependable transit times, and clear coordination across European routes.

Tautliner Transport Services

With a strong specialization in tautliner transport, we handle a wide range of cargo types requiring flexible loading and secure handling. Our experience enables us to support diverse freight requirements while maintaining high safety and operational standards.

Cross-Border EU Logistics

We manage cross-border transport operations with a thorough understanding of European logistics standards and regulatory requirements. Our focus is on smooth coordination, compliance, and reliable execution across EU markets.

Flexible Fleet & Partner-Based Network

CargoMate operates with a flexible, partner-based fleet model that allows us to adapt quickly to changing transport demands. By collaborating with trusted carriers, we ensure scalability, reliability, and consistent service quality.

Customized Transport Solutions

Every client and shipment presents unique requirements. We work closely with our partners to design tailored transport solutions aligned with specific routing, timing, and operational needs—always with a focus on efficiency, reliability, and transparency.

About Us

Our Company

CargoMate Cetem SL is an international road transport company built on a family heritage of over three decades in European freight operations, with a strong specialization in tautliner-based road transport.

Our Heritage

Our family has been active in the road transportation sector since 1991, developing deep operational expertise and a reputation for reliability across major European routes. Founded as a second-generation initiative, CargoMate represents the evolution of that legacy—combining established industry know-how with a modern, technology-driven approach to logistics and transport management.

Our Approach

We operate as a modern logistics partner, integrating digital tools, structured workflows, and data-informed decision-making into every stage of the transport process. This enables us to deliver efficient, transparent, and dependable transport solutions for our clients.

Our Values

  • Reliability and operational discipline
  • Clear communication and transparency
  • Long-term partnerships built on trust
  • Continuous improvement and adaptability

Looking Ahead

CargoMate aims to grow as an independent transport company while contributing to the long-term development of the broader family business ecosystem—maintaining the standards and values that have shaped our operations for more than 30 years.
